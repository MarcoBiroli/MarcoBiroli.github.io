2021

Number of distinct sites visited by a resetting random walker. M. Biroli, F. Mori. S. Majumdar.

Paper in writing which is the result of my M1 internship with Pr. Majumdar. In this paper we detail the derivation of the number of distinct visited sites by a resetting random walker. We derive the exact distribution in arbitrary positive real dimension as well as it's asymptotic behavior at long times. In one dimension we were able to derive a closed form expression for the full distribution.

2020

Time-Privacy-Robustness-Accuracy Trade Offs for OV-Net.

Paper in writing which started as a project for the Computer Science class of Pr. David Naccache "Computer Science through Practice". In this paper we endow an existing voting protocol: OV-Net, with mechanics aiming at countering it's weakness with regard to unresponsive participants.
