Short summary of studies and interest.

I am currently a Master 1 student in the Ecole Normale Superieure (ENS), rue d'Ulm, International Centre for Fundamental Physics (IFCP) Master program. You can find a detail of the program here. I did my Bachelor at Ecole Polytechnique from 2017/2018 to 2020 where I studied the fundamentals of Physics, Mathematics and Computer Science. I am still discovering a lot of new aspects in physics but for the moment the subjects that interest me most are the ones which relate to: out of equilibrium physics, random graph theory and general relativity. More generally the 'techniques'/'tools' that I enjoy using the most are: statistical physics, probability, differential geometry and numerical methods.
While it is true that I have spent most of my life in France I have always been part of a multi-cultural and international environment. I was in an international highschool, the Bachelor at Ecole Polytechnique is an English international program and so is the ICFP Master I am currently enrolled in. I am fluent in English, French and Italian and I speak some Spanish.
As for non-physics related interests, I love fencing, skiing, sailing and chess and I am currently testing my neighbours resilience by learning how to play violin.
