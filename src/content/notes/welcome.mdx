The old site had served me well since grad school, but it had become a wall of bullet points. This rebuild has a simpler goal: put the work first, then surround it with the occasional explainer.

Expect two flavors of posts here:

1. **Short notes** — reading notes, conference write-ups, the odd opinion.
2. **Research explainers** — illustrated, interactive walk-throughs of my own papers.

If you'd like to get in touch, email is the fastest way.
